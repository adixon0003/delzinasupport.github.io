Our Mission

At Delzina Support, our mission is simple: to provide dependable, compassionate, person‑centered support that empowers individuals with disabilities to live with dignity, independence, and joy.

With over 30 years of care experience in Tampa Bay — and more than a decade dedicated exclusively to disability support — founder Laketa Tillman brings unmatched commitment to every family she serves.

Our Services

Personal Support

Daily living assistance that promotes independence and safety.

Companion Services

Social engagement, community outings, and emotional support.

Respite Care

Life Skills Training

Safe, dependable rides to appointments, activities, and community events.
