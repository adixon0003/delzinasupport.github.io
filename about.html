About Delzina Support

Reliable care rooted in experience and compassion.

Our Story

Delzina Support was founded on a simple belief: every individual deserves compassionate, reliable care that honors their dignity and supports their independence.

With over 30 years of experience providing care in the Tampa Bay region — and more than a decade dedicated exclusively to supporting individuals with disabilities — Laketa Tillman brings a rare combination of expertise, patience, and heart.

Families trust Laketa because she leads with compassion, communicates with clarity, and shows up with consistency.

Mission

To empower individuals with disabilities through compassionate, person‑centered support that enhances independence, dignity, and quality of life.

Vision

A community where every person — regardless of ability — has the support, respect, and opportunities they need to thrive.

Our Values

  • Compassion — We lead with empathy and understanding.
  • Trust — Families rely on us because we show up, every time.
  • Respect — Every person is treated with dignity and care.
  • Consistency — Dependable support is the foundation of our work.
  • Community — We believe in building strong, supportive connections.
